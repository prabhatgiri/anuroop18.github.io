Electric propulsion: The future of aerospace propulsion.

Various methods are used for propulsion of rockets, spacecrafts and other aerospace vehicles. Our of them, electrical methods are most elegant. But until now, there have not been enough advancements in electric propulsion technology, so that they can be used to propel any aerospace vehicle. For now their use is limited to providing very small thrusts to satellites in space. Here we explore electrical propulsion in most detailed form possible.
